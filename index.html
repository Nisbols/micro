Soft Boiled Eggs

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • salt
  • water

tools

  • pot
  • stove
  • bowl

Steps

  1. Put water in pot
  2. Add salt to pot
  3. Put stove on high and heat water to a boil
  4. add eggs and boil for 8 minutes
  5. Remove eggs from pot and place in bowl with cold water
  6. once eggs cool down, enjoy!
